Hi there

Appyling to college is stressful. We know that. And we're here to help. With College Collab, our users can streamline their college application process. Serving as an intermediary to the Common Application portal, with College Collab, students can collect all the relevant documents they need for their application and get them approved by their counsellors.

Features

College Collab is a one-stop portal for students and counsellors to stay on top of college application deadlines. We help you make the most out of your application process!

  • Add essays, activities, standardised testing scores, transcripts, a college list, recommenders, and deadlines to a student's profile.
  • Invite individual students to your counselling groups.
  • Send your students notes.
  • View your students' progress on their applications.
  • View important components of your students' applications and make sure they're up to par.
